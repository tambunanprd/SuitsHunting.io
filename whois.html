Cheers!

Hi everyone, welcome to suitshunting.io, a platform initiated by Andi Parada Tambunan, with the spirit of sharing knowledge in the fields of Threat Hunting, Malware Analysis, and Security Operation Center (SOC). This platform is built on the enthusiasm of a cybersecurity researcher who is not just a user of tools but also an innovator in creating and understanding cybersecurity strategies.

It all started from our basecamp, known as "Ruko Lab", a place where cybersecurity enthusiasts gather to conduct in-depth research, share insights, and develop new approaches to tackling cybersecurity threats. From this basecamp, the idea emerged to share our research findings and knowledge with a wider community, with the hope of building a stronger and more collaborative cybersecurity ecosystem.

At suitshunting.io, we believe that knowledge should continuously evolve and be shared. We are committed to providing the latest information, tools, and investigative reports related to threat hunting, malware analysis, and best practices in SOC management. This is not just a platform – it's a community for researchers, professionals, and anyone who wants to explore the world of cybersecurity with a more in-depth and analytical approach.
